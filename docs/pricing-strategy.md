**Prepared for:** SOZO Brain Center Board of Directors

**Status:** Draft for Board Review

---

## Executive Summary

DeepSynaps Studio is the only clinical platform that unifies **all 16 neuromodulation modalities** with qEEG-guided targeting, evidence-graded protocols, and automated governance — replacing fragmented tools that each cover only one modality or one function. This pricing strategy positions DeepSynaps as a premium clinical decision-support platform priced below the total cost of the tools it replaces, while building toward the board-approved targets of **1M clinicians** and **10M patient/consumer users** over 3 years.

| VisualDx | Annual subscription | $399/yr individual | Dermatology-focused diagnostic tool |

**Key Insight:** A neuromodulation clinic currently pays $3,395+ for NeuroGuide, $559+ for UpToDate, $200+/mo for practice management, and still has no unified protocol generation system. DeepSynaps replaces all of these for a single subscription.

| Billing | Price |
|---|---|
| Monthly | **€49/month** per clinician |

**Justification:**
- Below UpToDate ($559/yr) while offering 10× more neuromodulation depth

- Above median healthcare SaaS ($29/mo) reflecting specialist clinical value

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Seed (Months 1–6)
- Launch **Explorer (free)** to build user base and credibility
- Offer **Clinician tier at 50% discount** (€24.50/mo) for first 500 sign-ups ("Founding Members")
- Target: TMS clinics, neurofeedback practitioners, brain centres
- Channels: SOZO Brain Center network, neurostimulation conferences, qEEG training courses

### Phase 2: Grow (Months 7–18)
- Full pricing rollout
- Launch **Clinic Pro** with qEEG upload feature as differentiator
- NHS pilot programme (UK) — subsidised Clinic Pro for 10 NHS trusts
- Academic partnerships (Oxford, UCL, King's College)
- Device manufacturer partnerships (Storz Medical, MagVenture, Vielight)

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 19–36)
- Enterprise sales team (US, EU, Middle East)
- Marketplace launch — clinicians sell custom protocols
- Data licensing to pharma companies for trial design
- White-label deals with device manufacturers
- API ecosystem for EHR integration partners

## Competitive Moat

| DeepSynaps Advantage | Nearest Competitor | Gap |
|---|---|---|
| 16 modalities in one platform | NeuroGuide (1 modality focus) | No competitor covers all modalities |
| qEEG → Protocol automation | Manual process (clinician knowledge) | First mover — no direct competitor |
| Evidence grading (EV-A–D) | UpToDate (general medicine) | Neuromodulation-specific grading doesn't exist elsewhere |
| Regulatory precision engine | None | FDA cleared ≠ approved distinction is unique |
| 3,000+ indexed papers | PubMed (manual search) | Curated, condition-mapped, evidence-linked |
| Governance + review queue | None in neuromodulation | Clinical safety layer is a regulatory selling point |
