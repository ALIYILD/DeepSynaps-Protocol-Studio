# DeepSynaps Studio — Expansion Candidates Report
**Database expansion candidates ranked by confidence and evidence grade**

> **Governance Policy:** All EV-C and EV-D candidates are flagged **CLINICIAN-ONLY REVIEW REQUIRED**. These entries must be reviewed and approved by a qualified clinician before database ingestion. EV-A and EV-B entries are high-confidence additions suitable for standard pipeline review.
>
> **Regulatory Terminology:**
> - "FDA cleared (510(k))" = substantial equivalence to a predicate device; does NOT mean safety/efficacy demonstrated under rigorous PMA standard
> - "FDA approved (PMA)" = premarket approval; highest regulatory standard
> - "FDA cleared (De Novo)" = novel low-to-moderate-risk device without a predicate
> - "CE-marked" = EU conformity declaration; does not imply FDA clearance
> - Device listing/registration ≠ cleared intended use
> - Flow FL-100 remains the **only** PMA-approved tDCS device
