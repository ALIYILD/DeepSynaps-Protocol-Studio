## **CORE NON-NEGOTIABLE PRINCIPLES**

### **NEVER DO:**
- ❌ Silently fall back
- ❌ Fabricate evidence
- ❌ Fabricate biomarker certainty
- ❌ Imply diagnosis
- ❌ Imply prescribing
- ❌ Imply calibrated prediction unless validated
- ❌ Hide degraded states
- ❌ Show fake demo data as real
- ❌ Allow frontend/backend contract drift

### **ALWAYS DO:**
- ✅ Label provenance
- ✅ Label demo/live
- ✅ Label heuristic/inferred/measured
- ✅ Preserve auditability
- ✅ Preserve consent enforcement
- ✅ Preserve clinic isolation
- ✅ Preserve clinician-review framing
- ✅ Preserve evidence traceability

### **GLOBAL UI STATE STANDARD:**

Every page must distinguish:
- **live** — Real patient data from EMR
- **demo** — Demonstration/training data
- **inferred** — Model predictions, algorithm outputs
- **heuristic** — Rule-based approximations
- **degraded** — Partial/incomplete/stale data
- **unavailable** — Feature not accessible
- **preview-only** — Development/preview state
- **partial** — Some data missing (specify what)
- **stale** — Data older than acceptable (specify age)
