# DeepSynaps Studio — Pricing & Packages

## Plans

| Plan | Price | Seats | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Explorer | Free | 1 | Evaluators exploring the platform |
| Resident / Fellow | $99 / month | 1 | Trainees and early-career clinicians |
| Clinician Pro | $199 / month | 1 | Independent clinicians |
| Clinic Team | $699 / month | Up to 10 | Clinical teams |
| Enterprise | From $2,500 / month | Unlimited | Organizations |

Annual pricing is available for Resident, Clinician Pro, and Clinic Team.
Enterprise pricing is custom.

---

## Feature Summary

### Explorer (Free)
- Evidence library — read access only
- Device registry — limited view
- Conditions and modalities — limited browsing
- No protocol generation, uploads, exports, or review queue

### Resident / Fellow ($99/mo)
- Full evidence library, device registry, conditions access
- Protocol generation — EV-A/B only, no off-label
- Assessment builder — limited
- Handbook generation — limited
- PDF export
- No uploads, no review queue, no team features

### Clinician Pro ($199/mo)
- Full protocol generator (EV-C with clinician override; EV-D always blocked)
- Uploads for qEEG / MRI / PDFs
- Personalized case summary
- Assessment builder — full
- Handbook generation — full
- PDF + DOCX export (patient-facing if governance allows)
- Personal review queue
- Personal audit trail
- Monthly monitoring digest
- Single user only
- Add-on: Phenotype mapping

### Clinic Team ($699/mo, up to 10 seats)
- Everything in Clinician Pro
- Phenotype mapping included
- Shared team review queue
- Team comments and assignments
- Full audit trail (personal + team)
- Team governance workflows
- Team templates
- Seat management
- Basic white-label branding
- Queue-fed monitoring updates

### Enterprise (from $2,500/mo, custom)
- Everything in Clinic Team
- Unlimited seats
- Advanced governance rules
- Full white-label branding
- API / integrations
- Automated monitoring workspace
- Advanced audit retention
- SSO-ready structure
- Data residency flags

---

## Governance Independence

**Commercial package tier does not affect governance restrictions.** The following rules
apply to all users regardless of plan:

- EV-D evidence is never included in patient-facing exports
- Off-label protocols always require a clinical review note
- EV-C evidence requires clinician override (Clinician Pro+)
- Regulatory language precision (FDA cleared vs approved) is enforced at all tiers
- Contraindication checks are mandatory for protocol activation

These restrictions cannot be relaxed by upgrading a plan.
